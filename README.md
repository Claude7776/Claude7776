```yaml
  focus:
    - "Secure & scalable infrastructure design"

    - "Cloud-native deployments & networking"
    - "Monitoring, observability & incident response"
    - "Blue Team defensive security practices"
  mindset: "Automate. Secure. Monitor. Repeat."
```

<br clear="right"/>

I design, secure, and automate infrastructure end-to-end — from bare-metal Linux servers and self-hosted networks to containerized cloud deployments and CI/CD pipelines. My work sits at the intersection of **DevOps engineering** and **defensive cybersecurity**, with a strong bias toward automation, observability, and reproducibility.

I enjoy building systems that don't just work — they **scale, self-heal, and stay secure**.
